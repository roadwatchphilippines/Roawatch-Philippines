FOR CITIZENS

How Citizens Make a Difference

Your voice helps protect your family, neighbors, and daily commuters.

  1. 📱
    Scan the QR Code Spot RoadWatch PH QR codes in barangay halls, waiting sheds, or electric posts to quickly open the website.
  2. 📝
    Add Details, Snap a Photo, and Pin Tap Report Road Issue, complete the Google Form, upload a clear photo, and pinpoint the location on the map.
  3. 📬
    Submit and Track Submit instantly and check your email for a unique tracking number to monitor status updates in real time.

FOR AUTHORITIES

How Authorities Drive Solutions

Real-time coordination helps teams respond faster where help is needed most.

  1. 🖥️
    Receive Reports Barangay, LGU, and DPWH teams receive incoming reports through the RoadWatch PH website in real time.
  2. 🚦
    Verify and Prioritize Officials validate submitted reports, assess severity, and prioritize urgent road concerns for faster action.
  3. 🔧
    Update Progress Repair teams update statuses like Pending, In Progress, and Repaired so citizens stay informed.
  4. 🤝
    Ensure Transparency Digital tracking keeps the process accountable and helps communities see that road concerns are being addressed.

Every update matters. Citizens report, authorities act, and communities stay informed together.

What You Can Report

Citizens can report different types of road and infrastructure problems to help authorities respond faster and improve road safety.

🛣️ Road Surface Problems

  • Potholes
  • Road cracks
  • Faded or missing road markings
  • Uneven or damaged road surfaces

🌧️ Drainage and Flooding Problems

  • Clogged roadside drainage
  • Missing or broken drainage cover
  • Flooded roads during heavy rain
  • Water pooling on the road

⚠️ Road Safety Issues

  • Malfunctioning traffic lights
  • Missing or damaged traffic signs
  • Lack of pedestrian crossings
  • Broken or missing guardrails

🏙️ Street Infrastructure Problems

  • Damaged sidewalk
  • Broken streetlights
  • Damaged road reflectors
  • Open or uncovered manholes

🚧 Road Obstruction Issues

  • Fallen trees blocking the road
  • Construction materials on the road
  • Illegal parking blocking lanes
  • Debris or large objects on the roadway

Every report helps agencies prioritize road repairs and improve safety for all road users.
