Our purpose is to help citizens report common road issues quickly so local authorities can respond faster and keep roads safer for everyone.

How to Report (Location Step-by-Step)

Follow these guided steps to capture and submit the exact coordinates of a road issue.

  1. 📍 Enter the LocationOpen the location picker from the report flow.
  2. 🧭 Location Tool opensYou will be redirected to the location selection screen.
  3. ⚙️ Choose detection modeUse Auto Detect Location (GPS) or Search for Location (road, barangay, landmark).
  4. 🗺️ Review generated coordinatesThe system shows your Latitude and Longitude.
  5. 📋 Copy CoordinatesTap the copy button to save the generated values.
  6. ↩️ Paste to report formReturn and paste into the Location Coordinates field.
  7. ✅ Fill Out the Report NowComplete the remaining details and submit your report.

What You Can Report

Citizens can report different types of road and infrastructure problems to help authorities respond faster and improve road safety.

🛣️ Road Surface Problems

  • Potholes
  • Road cracks
  • Faded or missing road markings
  • Uneven or damaged road surfaces

🌧️ Drainage and Flooding Problems

  • Clogged roadside drainage
  • Missing or broken drainage cover
  • Flooded roads during heavy rain
  • Water pooling on the road

⚠️ Road Safety Issues

  • Malfunctioning traffic lights
  • Missing or damaged traffic signs
  • Lack of pedestrian crossings
  • Broken or missing guardrails

🏙️ Street Infrastructure Problems

  • Damaged sidewalk
  • Broken streetlights
  • Damaged road reflectors
  • Open or uncovered manholes

🚧 Road Obstruction Issues

  • Fallen trees blocking the road
  • Construction materials on the road
  • Illegal parking blocking lanes
  • Debris or large objects on the roadway

Every report helps agencies prioritize road repairs and improve safety for all road users.
