RoadWatch PH

Sumbungan ng Bayan para sa Mas Maayos na Daan.

Our purpose is to help citizens report common road issues quickly so local authorities can respond faster and keep roads safer for everyone.

What You Can Report

  • Potholes and broken pavement
  • Flood-prone road sections
  • Damaged signs and lane markings
  • Unsafe road construction areas

Every report helps agencies prioritize road repairs and improve safety for all road users.

Common Issues in Roads

Potholes

Deep potholes can damage tires, cause accidents, and slow down traffic flow in busy roads.

Flooded Streets

Flooding reduces visibility, weakens pavement, and puts commuters at high risk during storms.

Road Cracks

Cracks spread over time and lead to more severe structural damage when left untreated.

Unsafe Construction Zones

Missing signs and barriers around repairs can endanger drivers, cyclists, and pedestrians.

Faded Lane Markings

Worn road markings create confusion, especially at night and during heavy rain conditions.

Rough Pavement

Uneven or broken asphalt can cause vehicle wear, discomfort, and reduced travel safety.
