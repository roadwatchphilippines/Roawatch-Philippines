How RoadWatch PH Works

RoadWatch PH allows residents to report damaged roads, potholes, cracks, flooding, and other hazards. Citizens submit reports with location details, issue descriptions, and photos. These reports help local authorities and communities identify urgent road concerns and prioritize repairs.

Purpose of RoadWatch PH

The purpose of RoadWatch PH is to improve road safety, reduce travel risks, and encourage civic participation. By giving people a simple way to report problems, the platform supports faster response, better road monitoring, and safer streets for everyone.
